# 👨‍💻 SOP: Finance User (Owner/Reviewer)

This SOP covers the daily activities for a Finance Team Member.

---

## 1. Daily Morning Routine
1.  **Login**: Ensure you are in the correct "Workplace" view.
2.  **Check Notifications**: Look for the red "Pending" counts on the metric cards.
3.  **Daily Tasks**: Navigate to "Recurring Activities" -> "Daily Tasks." Mark all your daily items (EOD Reports, Cash reconciliations) as **Completed**.

---

## 2. Managing Your Tasks
1.  **View My Tasks**: Filter the dashboard to show "My Tasks."
2.  **Move to Review**: When a task is finished, click the status badge to move it to "Under Review." 
    *   *Note: You must assign a Reviewer if one isn't already assigned.*
3.  **Reviewing Others**: If a task is assigned to you for review, check the work and click "Fully Completed" or "Reject" if changes are needed.

---

## 3. Creating Ad-hoc Tasks
1.  Click **+ New Task**.
2.  Select the Entity, Task Name, and Due Date.
3.  Always assign an Owner and a Reviewer immediately.

---

## 4. Handling Payments
1.  Navigate to **Payments**.
2.  Find your pending payments for the week.
3.  Once the payment is made in the bank, click **Mark Paid**.
4.  Enter the **Actual Date** and **Amount Paid**.
5.  If there is a delay, use the **Hold** button and provide a reason.

---

## 5. Reporting Learnings (LO)
*   If you find a process gap or a recurring error, go to **Learning** and click **+ Add New Learning**.
*   This is not for blaming; it's for improving the process for the next time.
