# ⚖️ SOP: Authorized Allocator / Gatekeeper

Allocators are responsible for managing external departmental requests.

---

## 1. Monitoring Requests
1.  Navigate to **Workplace** -> **Allocations Hub** (or "All Requests").
2.  Look for requests with the status **"Pending"** (Orange badge).

---

## 2. Allocation Process
1.  Review the "Nature of Request" and the "Finance Function."
2.  Click the **"Convert to Task"** button.
3.  Assign the **Owner** (the person who will do the work) and the **Reviewer**.
4.  Set a **Due Date** based on the urgency.

---

## 3. Rejection Process
*   If a request is outside the scope of Finance or is missing critical information, click **Reject**.
*   Provide a clear reason in the Remarks so the requester knows what to fix.

---

## 4. Oversight
*   Check the "Under Process" count regularly to ensure no team member is overloaded.
*   Use the **Entity Filter** to see if specific entities have more pending requests than others.
